## Known Limitations

1. **SQL Injection**: 0% recall (20 samples too few)
2. **DoS attacks**: Often confused with each other (common in IDS research)
3. **Real data**: Not yet benchmarked (requires --download flag + hours of training)
4. **Class imbalance**: 600:1 ratio still challenges minority detection
